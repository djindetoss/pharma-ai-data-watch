ℹ️ About this portal

A focused resource at the
frontier of pharma and AI

Pharma AI & Data Watch is an independent intelligence portal dedicated to tracking developments at the intersection of artificial intelligence, data science, and pharmaceutical regulation. It brings together high-quality, vetted content from leading scientific journals, regulatory agencies, and industry events.

Coverage spans the full drug development lifecycle — from AI-assisted target identification and generative chemistry, through clinical trial innovation and real-world evidence, to EU and global regulatory science, data standards, and health data governance.

Content is selected for scientific and regulatory relevance, with a focus on accuracy and depth over volume. If you have a relevant publication, event, or resource to suggest, use the contact link below.
